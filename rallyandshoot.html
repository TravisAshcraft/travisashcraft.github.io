Rally and Shoot

This game is being developed using Unreal Engine 4.26 this is a unique game. Mixing genre's of RTS and chaotic Third Person Shooter with the ability for table top creators to add their battalions and fight against each other. This game is still in its early stages of development but has potential for changing they way people can play table top games.
