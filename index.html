Third Person Shooter

I created this third person shooter with Unreal Engine 4 and C++. The AI can find the player, chase the player, attack the player, and if player is out of sight the AI returns back to its starting point. The player has full control over the player character. I created place holder UI for the aim dot and health. Health bar does work, along with the sound effects looping through different sounds for the gun.
