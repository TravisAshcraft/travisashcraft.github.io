Unrela Engine 4 Begginer Tutorial

This is the first video of my mini-series to get new developers aquanited with Unreal Engine 4 and the layout. The main focus is to get a game built that they can share and show without a lot of hassel of getting into the niddy griddy of the engine. I remeber how overwhelmed I felt the first time I opened Unreal and began creating my first game in C++. It had long compile times and I would always end up with errors and want to quit. I want new beginners to understand that it can be frustrating but give them a win under their belt and slowly get more involved with the depth of game development.
