Rally and Blade

This game is being developed inside of Unreal Engine 4.24.4. I am over gameplay programming along with animation setups. Since this is a indie team project I wear many hats during the development of this project. This game is based off the gameplay of Mount and Blade: Warband. We plan to have directional blocking and directional attacking meaning the player will be able to swing from left or right with their mouse, same with the blocking. This game is also going to be available for multiplayer, allowing players to connect in teams of 4 and setup AI counterparts to place battles.
